    My Home IT LAB

    In order to satisfy the requirements of the capstone requirement at WVUP, I purchased and configured my own home IT lab. The finished project is demonstrated briefly in the video below but has since grown to include six routers, two physical hosts with over five virtual instances between them. The network itself makes use of concepts from the Cisco Networking Academy Curriculim (VLANS, NAT, Frame-Relay just to name a few). This project is a huge achievement for me as it earned me with my Associates Degree of Applied Science and has allowed for hours of fun since.
